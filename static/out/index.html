webrtcHandler.js

Using the WebRTC API, users are able to connect and exchange data directly in the browser. They are guided through a series of offers and answers to establish a peer connection. Once the connection is successfully made, the users are able to exchange data through a data channel. This includes audio, video, and chat messages.
