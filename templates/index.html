Welcome to StraightFace!

• StraightFace is a random video-chat roulette app where one of you will try and make the other person smile or laugh!

• Once the game starts, you will have 1 minute to win the round.

• If you're team Yellow, use your creativity, humor, and charisma to make the other player laugh or smile.

• If you're team Grey, your goal is to resist laughing or smiling! Even a slight grin might give it away.

• Log In or Sign Up to play with friends and keep track of your account stats!
