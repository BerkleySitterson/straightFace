How to Play


• StraightFace is a random video-chat roulette app where the goal is to make your opponent smile or be the one keeping a straight face!

• Once you choose your role, you will have 30 seconds to win the round.

• If you're the Joker, use your creativity, humor, and charisma to make the other player laugh or smile.

• If you're keeping straight face, your mission is to resist laughing or smiling during your turn.

• Good Luck and Have Fun!
